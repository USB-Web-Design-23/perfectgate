John McEnroe

John McEnroe was born in West Germany (his dad was stationed in the air force) and moved to New York at a young age. He went to Stanford University for a short time before turning professional. He is considered to be the best tennis player at volleying ever. He was extemely fiery and often would rant at the Chair Umpire or fellow players. He ended up winning 7 Grand Slam titles. After retiring, he has pursued a successful career in broadcasting.
